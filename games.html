Glithcoria

It is prototype of a 2D Metroidvania game. In Glitchoria you play as a character named Nollo and you have to fight enemies corrupted by glitches while using your own glitches as power. We used Unity. In this game I programed, did animation and worked on the audio. I used C# and worked on the save system, platforms, obstacles and implemented the audio and audio triggers. I also programed the menus and dialogue system. I did the animations of the enemies using 3ds Max and rendering it in pixel art. I worked on some audio effects using FL Studio and Reaper and using recorded sounds
